Title: Build Contact Page and Start the Conversation Form

## Parent

## What to build

Build the Contact page around the Start the Conversation pattern. The form should invite Organizational Leaders to describe the challenge they are trying to solve without asking them to estimate budget or timeline. eCongruity owns cost and timeline estimation as part of strategy and planning.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] The contact form includes name, email, organization, role or title, and what the visitor is trying to solve.
- [ ] The contact form does not include budget or timeline fields.
- [ ] Form labels are explicit and accessible.
- [ ] Required fields are clear without making the form feel like procurement paperwork.
- [ ] The page reinforces that the first step is a conversation about the challenge.
- [ ] The page is ready to wire into static-host form handling later without requiring a custom backend now.
